About Us: The Story Behind CodebyTanjil

Have you ever searched for a simple online calculator, only to be overwhelmed by clunky layouts, pop-up ads, and slow-loading websites? We know the struggle — and that’s exactly why CodebyTanjil was created.

From a Coder’s Frustration to a Community Toolkit

CodebyTanjil isn’t just another website. It’s a passion project built from personal experience. I’m Tanjil, the developer behind this platform. As someone who frequently needed quick calculations for coding, budgeting, or everyday tasks, I realized one thing — good tools were hard to find.

So, I built one myself.

What started as a few personal tools has now grown into a trusted suite of calculators used by students, developers, workers, and dreamers alike.
