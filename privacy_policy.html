Cookies

Like most sites, CodeByTanjil uses cookies. Cookies are small files stored on your device. They help us:

  • Remember your tool inputs (like values you type in)

  • Understand how people use our calculators

You can block cookies in your browser settings. Blocking cookies may stop some parts of the site from working.

We may use third-party tools for:

These services may collect their own info, such as cookies or user data. We do not control how they use your data. Please check their privacy policies for details.
